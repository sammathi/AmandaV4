Five things about Amanda Duffy, aka A. G. Duffy, aka Rat Under Paper.

  1. She is a Winnipeg-born and Toronto-based old-school (that is, paint on paper) illustrator.
  2. She enjoys the usual illustrative pursuits and artistic challenges, but depicts her foes in compromising situations if given the chance.
  3. Her client list is huge. You may ask for it; she is easy to get along with.
  4. She lives with a lovely family and the third in a succession of bull terriers.
  5. She has a typeface based on her hand-lettering at ShinnType.com – it’s fab.
